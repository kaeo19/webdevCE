Notably famous signers include:

Thomas Jefferson (Virginia)

Primary author of the Declaration; later the third U.S. President.

Benjamin Franklin (Pennsylvania)

Inventor, diplomat, and statesman; the oldest signer at age 70.

John Adams (Massachusetts)

Key advocate for independence; later the second U.S. President.

Samuel Adams (Massachusetts)

Organizer of the Boston Tea Party and revolutionary firebrand.
