Famous signers

Thomas Jefferson
He was from Virginia.
He wrote most of the Declaration of Independence.
Later, he became the 3rd President of the United States.
He believed strongly in freedom and people's rights.
John Adams
He was from Massachusetts.
He helped debate and push for independence.
Later, he became the 2nd President of the United States.
He was known for being honest and a strong leader.
Benjamin Franklin
He was from Pennsylvania.
He was a famous inventor, writer, and diplomat.
He helped get support from France during the war.
People liked him for his wisdom and clever ideas.
John Hancock
He was from Massachusetts.
He was the first person to sign the Declaration.
His signature is very large—that’s why people say "Put your John Hancock" when signing something.
He was also the president of the Continental Congress.
