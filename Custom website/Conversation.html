Conversation Starters & Responses

Use the left column for friendly openers and the right column for thoughtful, engaging replies.

Conversation Starters

  • What’s something you’re excited about right now?
  • Have you read or watched anything good lately?
  • What’s a place you love to spend time in and why?
  • What’s a hobby you’d recommend trying?
  • If you could learn one new skill instantly, what would it be?

Good Responses

  • That sounds really interesting — tell me more about why you like it.
  • I haven’t seen that — what did you enjoy most about it?
  • I can see why that place feels special; what’s your favorite memory there?
  • That sounds like a fun challenge — how did you get started with it?
  • That would be amazing to learn — what would you do first with that skill?
